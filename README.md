## Delivery Approach

```
01  Understand the business goal
    └── What problem are we solving? What does success look like?

02  Convert goal → clear project requirements
    └── No ambiguity. No assumptions left open.

03  Break requirements → actionable tasks
    └── Structured. Prioritised. Assigned.

04  Coordinate developers · designers · marketers · stakeholders
    └── Everyone aligned. Everyone unblocked.

05  Manage workflow, timelines, reviews, and blockers
    └── Proactive communication. Decisions made early.

06  Track progress via Git, tasks, docs, and communication
    └── Full visibility at every stage.

07  Review quality before delivery
    └── QA is built in throughout — not bolted on at the end.

08  Prepare for launch
    └── Checklists, sign-offs, go-live readiness.

09  Monitor results and improve post-launch
    └── Ship, learn, iterate.
```
